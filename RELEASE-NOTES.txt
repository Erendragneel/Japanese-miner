Japanese Miner v6.5.3

EXPEDITION SKILL PROGRESSION
- Every mine now has exactly four learning stops:
  1. Vocabulary
  2. Kanji
  3. Grammar
  4. Reading
- Each stop displays its own 0–100% progress.
- The mine summary displays all four skill percentages.
- Stops unlock sequentially as the previous skill reaches 100%.
- Clicking a stop launches questions filtered to that skill.
- The guardian unlocks only after all four skills reach 100%.
- Existing question statistics automatically populate the new percentages.

Japanese Miner v6.5.2

NEW: Full-Screen Guardian Trials
- Clicking Start Timed Trial immediately opens a dedicated boss page.
- A giant stage-specific guardian appears behind the questions.
- Live 1:00 countdown timer with a red final-10-second warning.
- Displays Question X of 25, correct-answer count, and progress bar.
- Correct answers automatically advance to the next question.
- One incorrect answer or running out of time fails the trial.
- Results page shows PASS or FAIL, score, questions answered, completion time, reward, and retry/return controls.
- Passing unlocks the next mine and awards Nuggets.
